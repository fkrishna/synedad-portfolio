Synedad also known as Yassin Hedad Abdella is a rapper, singer, and songwriter from the island of Haïti. Synedad was born in New York during the 1990’s to a Haitian mother and an Eritrean father. After his birth, his family went back to Haïti where he grew up and lived for the majority of his life. At a young age, Synedad discovered his passion for music and started recording himself making rap songs.

Years later, after one Studio Album “Avec Le Temps”, two EP’s “Pou Lokal Yo” and “Pou Lokal Yo Vol.2”, the young Haitian keeps surprising his audience with a growing catalog of various genres such as Rap, Reggea, Kompa, R&B and many more. The rapper always says “I can’t limit myself to a single genre or musical style, art has no boundaries and I’m taking full advantage of that. Always surprising my audience with new melodies accompanied by different languages and sounds. I guess it really is about the vibe, the vibe is simply the source of my music”.
